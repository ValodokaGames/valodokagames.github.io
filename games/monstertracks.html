MONSTER TRACKS

About the Game

Monster Tracks is a thrilling physics-based driving game where you pilot a powerful monster truck over treacherous terrain. Reach the finish line without flipping over! With insane obstacles and crumbling bridges, every level is a test of balance and throttle control.

Controls

Drive Forward: D or →

Drive Backward: A or ←

Restart Level: R
