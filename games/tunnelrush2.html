TUNNEL RUSH 2

About the Game

Step into the next generation of high-speed racing! Tunnel Rush 2 is the direct sequel to the world-famous original, bringing more color, more speed, and more complexity to the table. Unlike the first game, you now have to focus on color-coded obstacles. Smash through sections that match your current hue to gain massive points and survive the increasingly narrow corridors.

As you progress through the kaleidoscopic levels, you'll collect diamonds. Use these to unlock a massive hangar of new ships, each with unique aesthetics and trails. Whether you're dodging neon spikes or rotating gears, Tunnel Rush 2 tests your reaction time and visual focus like no other game.

Controls

Rotate Tunnel Left: A or ←

Rotate Tunnel Right: D or →

Pause Game: P or Esc
